Don's Sweet Tea

Recipe

Discription

A simple sweet tea recipe that anyone can do. Made by my great grandma, I remeber always wanting more of this treat. Go ahead and try it out!

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water

  • 4 family-sized tea bags

  • 2 cups white sugar

Steps to prepare:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot. Dissolve sugar in boiling water; remove from heat.

  2. Steep tea bags in water to desired strength of tea, 3 to 5 minutes.

  3. Refrigerate tea until cold, at least 1 hour.
